Paying for Your Procedure

We offer convenient financing for your investment

Here at Anderson Sobel Cosmetic Surgery, we strive to make payment for services clear and easy and we are happy to help you decide how best to pay for your cosmetic procedures, should you have questions. Payments are accepted in the form of cash, cashier's check, money order, or credit card. For your convenience, we accept all major credit cards.

For the convenience of our patients, we also offer financing through Care Credit,American HealthCare Lending, and United Medical Credit. These three companies offer convenient financing solutions for patients by providing extended financing options, low monthly payments, and convenient customer service. If you would like to apply for financing, please contact our office using the mini contact form to the right to get started! It’s very easy to apply!
