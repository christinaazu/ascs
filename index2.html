About Dr. Sobel

Dr. Alexander Sobel is an award-winning, double board certified surgeon in cosmetic surgery and Otolaryngology (facial cosmetic surgery) who has performed thousands of cosmetic procedures.

Dr. Sobel's private operating suite is accredited by the Accreditation Association for Ambulatory Health Care (AAAHC).

Drina Morga, Patient Coordinator

Drina Morga joins Anderson Sobel Cosmetic Surgery with over twenty years in the beauty industry. As Dr. Sobel's Patient Coordinator, she will guide you through your cosmetic surgery journey one step at a time.
