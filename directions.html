Directions to our Bellevue Location

From the South:

Take I-405 North to Bellevue. Take the NE 4th Street Exit (Exit 13A) and take a right on NE 4th Street. At the next stop light (116th Avenue NE), take a left. Continue on 116th Avenue NE for approximately 1 mile. You will pass some auto dealerships and eventually Overlake Hospital. Once you cross NE 12th Street, our office is the 4th building on the right. The address is 1632 116th Avenue NE, Suite A, Bellevue, WA 98004

From the North:

Take I-405 South to Bellevue, Take the NE 8th Street Exit (Exit 13B). The exit splits into East and West – take East NE 8th Street. Immediately get into the left lane and take a left on 116th Avenue NE. Continue northbound on 116th Avenue NE for about 1/2 mile. You will pass Overlake Hospital on the left. Once you cross NE 12th Street, our office will be the 3rd building on the right. Our address is 1632 116th Avenue NE, Suite A, Bellevue, WA 98004

From Seattle Over the 520 Bridge:

Drive East over the 520 Evergreen Point Floating Bridge. Once you cross over the bridge, take the 2nd exit which is Bellevue Way NE. You will then be driving southbound on Bellevue Way. At the 2nd main intersection, which is NE 12th Street, take a left. Continue on NE 12th Street and drive over the 405 freeway. Once you’ve crossed over the 405 freeway, take a left at 116th Avenue NE. Our office is the 3rd building on the right. Our address is 1632 116th Avenue NE, Suite A, Bellevue, WA 98004

From Seattle Over the I-90 Bridge:

Drive East over the I-90 Bridge and then take I-405 North to Bellevue. Take the NE 4th Street (Exit 13A). Take a right on NE 4th Street and then take a left on 116th Avenue NE and drive approximately one mile. You will pass auto dealerships and eventually Overlake Hospital. Once you cross NE 12th Street, our office is the 3rd building on the right. Our address is 1632 116th Avenue NE, Suite A, Bellevue, WA 98004
